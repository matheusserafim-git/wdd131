About Discover Porto Alegre

Porto Alegre is the capital and largest city of the Brazilian state of Rio Grande do Sul. Known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and beautiful landscapes, Porto Alegre offers a unique blend of urban sophistication and natural beauty.
