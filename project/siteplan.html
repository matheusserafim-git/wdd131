site name

Discover Porto Alegre

The name "Discover Porto Alegre" was chosen because it conveys the site's main objective: to present residents and visitors with the main tourist attractions, natural landmarks, historical sites, and cultural experiences of the city of Porto Alegre.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital tourist guide for Porto Alegre, offering information about attractions, historical sites, parks, gastronomy, and events.

Scenarios

  • What are the best places to visit in Porto Alegre during the weekend?
  • Where can I find information about the schedules and activities of Ipanema Beach?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#2E7D32): headings, menu, and buttons.
  • Red (#FF5252): links and highlights.

Typography

  • Poppins: titles.
  • Open Sans: body text.
  • Montserrat: buttons and highlights.
